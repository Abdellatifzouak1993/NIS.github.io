Who we are?

Newcastle International School is part of Newcastle International Academy since October 2017. We pride ourselves as one of the leading language schools in the northeast of England. We are accredited by The British Accreditation Council (BAC), which is an independent organisation, established to be the national accrediting body for independent post-16 education in the UK. We offer a wide range of courses from General English, General IELTS and Exam Preparation Courses, to popular modern language classes (Spanish, French, Italian, Arabic and Chinese), and English for Academic Purposes (Business, Law, and Medicine). Our courses are available on campus (Newcastle Upon Tyne, Blandford Square) and online
